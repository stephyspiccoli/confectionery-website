History

Coco’s Chocolatiers is a manufacturer of premium chocolates and related products. Coco’s chocolatiers was founded in Belgium in 1936. Coco’s Chocolatiers owns and operates more than 600 retail boutiques and shops in the United States, Canada, Europe, and Asia and is available via over 10,000 speciality retailers.

Chocolate

Coco’s Chocolatiers in addition to chocolates, also sells truffles, coffee, cocoa, biscuits, dipped fruits and sweets, chocolate liquor, shakes, candy, wedding and party favours and other items arranged in gift baskets. We also also produce seasonal and limited-edition chocolates with special packaging for major holidays. Coco’s Chocolatiers also has license agreements for the production of ice cream, cheesecake, coffee pods and liqueur that comes in several chocolate-related flavours. Products are also available in sugar-free and Kosher varieties.
