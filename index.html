History

Coco’s Chocolatiers is a manufacturer of premium chocolates and related products. Coco’s chocolatiers was founded in Belgium in 1936. Coco’s Chocolatiers owns and operates more than 600 retail boutiques and shops in the United States, Canada, Europe, and Asia and is available via over 10,000 speciality retailers. In addition to chocolates, Coco’s Chocolatiers also sells truffles, coffee, cocoa, biscuits, dipped fruits and sweets, chocolate liquor, shakes, candy, wedding and party favours and other items arranged in gift baskets. We also also produce seasonal and limited-edition chocolates with special packaging for major holidays. Coco’s Chocolatiers also has license agreements for the production of ice cream, cheesecake, coffee pods and liqueur that comes in several chocolate-related flavours. Products are also available in sugar-free and Kosher varieties.

Delicacies

Chocolate

Our specialty: Chocolate. We have been producing premium chocolates for over 80 years, and our chocolate has just gotten better. We believe chocolate should be more than just something you eat after lunch as dessert or as a snack. We want it to be an experience, an experience you will never forget. Coco’s Chocolatiers has Dark, Milk, White and our famous assorted chocolates. With these amazing delicacies we also produce our own hot chocolate, and this will for sure be the best experience of your life.

Coffee

Our roasted coffee beans are sure a great pleasure in a cold winter morning and in a hot summer day. With more than 10 different coffee flavours and specialty holiday beans we are sure to guarantee the best experience when it come to that first cup of coffee in the morning..
